About Price Prediction Tool

Welcome to our Product Price Prediction application. This tool uses machine learning technology to predict product prices based on various order factors and metrics.

Machine Learning Powered

Our price prediction system uses XGBoost, a powerful machine learning algorithm that analyzes multiple data points to generate accurate price predictions.

Real-time Analysis

Get instant price predictions based on your input data. Our model processes your information in real-time and delivers results within seconds.

Comprehensive Data Analysis

Our system considers multiple factors including order subtotal, taxes, shipping methods, and regional variations to provide accurate price estimates.

Secure and Private

Your data security is our priority. All information processed through our system remains confidential and is used solely for prediction purposes.
